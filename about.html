About CS Careers

Built by students, for students.

We're the largest student-led tech community at Virginia Tech. No recruiters. No fluff. Just people who've been where you are, helping you get where you want to go.

Our Mission

A launchpad, not a lecture hall.

To foster a robust educational space where students interested in computer science can flourish on their journey to the job market. We believe the path from classroom to career shouldn't feel like a maze — it should feel like momentum.

Everything we do — mentorship circles, mock interviews, company nights, project sprints — ladders up to one question: "Did this get a member one step closer?"

What We Do

Three pillars, one community.

01

Mentorship

Upperclassmen and alumni paired with underclassmen. Office hours, résumé reviews, real talk.

02

Industry Access

Monthly company nights, tech talks, and direct intro lines to recruiters at 12+ partner companies.

03

Build Together

Project sprints, hackathon teams, and study groups — because the fastest way to learn is to ship.
